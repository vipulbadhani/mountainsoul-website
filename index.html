What We Do

Our Programs & Initiatives

Bridging modern education with ancient wisdom — making villages living classrooms and women the real knowledge holders of Devbhoomi Uttarakhand.

📚
01
Modern Mind, Ancient Roots
Our flagship experiential learning program takes school students to villages where they learn Biology, Chemistry, Physics and Social Science through traditional practices — aligned with NEP 2020 and Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS).
👩‍🌾
02
Women & SHG Empowerment
Village women of Saura Sari SHG are educators and leaders — teaching seed preservation, pickle making, leaf-plate (pattal) manufacturing, and rope making. Their knowledge earns dignity and income.
🏅
03
Sports & Life Discipline
In partnership with MP Sports College, Dehradun — students experience football grounds, hockey fields, athletic tracks, shooting ranges, and Asia's first ice skating rink to build character, discipline, and resilience.
🌿
04
Sustainability & Culture
100+ trees planted across 9 villages, Ganga Swachhata Abhiyan, Giloy Kadha distribution during COVID, and campaigns preserving Uttarakhand's festivals, food traditions, and indigenous knowledge systems.

How We Work

The Village as a Living School

Learning by doing — an ancient and global philosophy. Every village is a unique education centre with teachers who have never been given a classroom.

01
Village as Classroom
Students are taken to real villages where farmers, women SHGs and elders become the teachers — reversing the traditional knowledge hierarchy.
02
Kishan Ki Pathsala
Farmers teach full crop cycles, traditional irrigation (Gharat, Panchakki), soil health, natural fertilizers and weather reading — ancient science made alive.
03
Culture Through Living
Students taste Jhangora and Mandua, wear traditional dress, witness rope-making and pattal crafts — culture is not taught as nostalgia, it is lived.
04
The Gift of Dignity
Each student receives a Gift Pack made by SHG women — pickles, handicrafts and leaf plates — creating market linkage between village producers and urban consumers.

About Us

Born in the Toughest Time

Mountain Soul Foundation was born during COVID-19 in 2021. Manoj Prakash Jaguri, a native of Uttarkashi, inspired by his mother's resilience and the mountains' ancient wisdom, began distributing Giloy Kadha village to village. That act of care became a movement — and a registered NGO by October 2021.

"The soul of the mountains can only be protected by empowering the people who live there."
— Manoj Prakash Jaguri, Founder & President
  • Founded 2021 — born during COVID-19, registered NGO October 2021
  • Active across Garhwal & Kumaon regions of Uttarakhand
  • Aligned with NEP 2020 and Indian Knowledge Systems (IKS)
  • Partner: MP Sports College Dehradun, Bright Public Junior High School
  • Programs: Education · Women Empowerment · Sports · Sustainability · Culture
